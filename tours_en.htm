 Tours

1st Route – Tour Of Kastoria

Starting from the city center we are heading to Dolco where we find mansions from the 17th and 18th century A.D. Ongoing we find Dragon’s cave, Panagia Mavriotissa, Ai Sotiras and to the city hall where we can see a part of the city walls. Going up to Mitropoleos street we find a lot of the 80 byzantine churches. Some of the churches are: TaxiarchisMitropoleos (10th century) where the bones of PavlosMelas and his wife Nataly are being kept, St Nickolas of Kansitzi (12th century) at omoniasquare, Panagia Koumbelidiki (10 century) which is the emblem of Kastoria,the church St. Stefanos (9th century), the church of St. Anargiri (11th century). We continue towards Prophet Helias and St. Athanasios with the great panoramic view. We end our route to the ring road with the extraordinary view of the city.
Route Duration: with 1 hour wait and return to the center.
Cost: 25 euros

2nd Route – Historical Route

In a distance of 5 kilometers from Kastoria we find Dispilio village where at 1932 was discovered a lake settlement by Professor A. Keramopoulos. The digging started at 1992 from the professor of prehistoric archeology G. Hournouziadis and his team. The settlement is dated 7000 years ago and represents the way of life of the first inhabitants of Kastoria. One of the most important things that was discovered is a wooden sign that has carved symbols from a primitive form of writing. Leaving Dispilio we are heading to Argos Orestiko where we visit the local Archeological museum. We continue 15 kilometers away from Argos Orestiko, at 900 meters altitude, to Nostimo village in which was discovered a petrified forest dated about 20.000.000 years ago with tropical and subtropical plants, palm trees and fossils of sea life creatures and prehistoric animals.
Route Duration: with return 2 hours and 2 hour wait.
Cost: 55 euros

3rd Route – St. Anna – Nestorio

Starting from the city of Kastoria on route to Nestorio we make a right turn to St. Anna village. At the end of the village there is the monastery of ZoodochouPigis, in a majestic scenery where lies St. Anna’s waterfall. Ongoing we head to Nestorio which is at the foothills of mount Grammos enjoying food and coffee by the banks of Aliakmonas river at the facilities of River Party festival.
Route Duration: with return 1 hour and 1 hour wait.
Cost: 40 euros

4th Route – Prespes

This tour includes sightseeing at the beautiful villages of Prespes along with a tour at the wetlands of small and great Prespa.
Route Duration: with return 1 hour and 30 minutes and 3 hours wait.
Cost: 90 euros

5th Route – Nympheon

In a distance of 60 kilometers from Kastoria crossing the passage of Klisoura lies the village Nympheon. With the efforts of locals a lot of houses has been reconstructed and now works as guesthouses. At the village there is the brown bear refuge, where we can observe the bears at their natural habitat, thanks to the organization Arcturos.
Route Duration: with return 1 hour and 30 minutes and 3 hours wait.
Cost: 80 euros

6th Route – Monastery Sightseeing

Starting from Kastoria we are heading towards Korisos. Right after Korisos we find St. Nikolaos monastery. Ongoing to Lithia we make a left turn to Melissotopos where we find St. Anargiri monastery which is reported on church texts back from 1457 and at the 18th century A.D. was a significant religious center. After that we are heading to women’s monastery of St. Paraskevi.
Route Duration: with return 1 hour and 1 hour wait.
Cost: 40 euros
